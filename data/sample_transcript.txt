Acme Corp Q2 FY2024 Earnings Call Transcript (Illustrative sample)

Operator: Good afternoon, and welcome to Acme Corp's second quarter fiscal 2024
earnings conference call. Prepared remarks will be followed by a Q&A session.

CEO — Prepared Remarks:
Thank you, everyone. I'm pleased to report another strong quarter. Revenue grew 14%
year over year, and we saw particular strength in our subscription business as net
new customer additions accelerated. Given the momentum we're seeing in the pipeline
and our improving win rates, we are raising our full-year revenue guidance. We feel
more confident today than we did a quarter ago about our ability to execute.

CFO — Prepared Remarks:
Gross margin expanded roughly 300 basis points year over year, driven by cloud cost
optimization and disciplined pricing. Operating margin also improved as revenue grew
faster than operating expenses. We remain focused on durable, profitable growth.

Q&A:
Analyst: Can you talk about customer concentration?
CFO: Our top three customers remain a meaningful portion of revenue. We are actively
diversifying the base, and net new logo growth this quarter is a direct result of
that effort.

Analyst: Any comment on international exposure?
CFO: International is our fastest-growing region, which does increase our sensitivity
to currency movements. We manage that exposure actively.
