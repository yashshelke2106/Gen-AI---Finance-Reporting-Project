Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations

Results of Operations. Total revenue increased 13.6% year over year, driven primarily
by growth in our subscription segment as we added net new logos and expanded seats
within existing accounts. Professional services revenue was roughly flat. Gross margin
improved to 44.0% from 41.0% a year ago, reflecting cloud infrastructure cost
optimization and modest price increases.

Liquidity and Capital Resources. We ended the quarter with a strong balance sheet and
believe our current cash and cash flow from operations are sufficient to fund
operations for at least the next twelve months.

Item 1A. Risk Factors

Our three largest customers accounted for a significant portion of revenue, and the
loss of any of them could materially harm our results. In addition, an increasing
share of revenue is generated internationally, exposing us to foreign currency
exchange rate fluctuations.
